FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions
Asked Questions

Have Any Question On Minds! Asked Question

What services does Safeway courier Service Co. LTD. offer?

Safeway courier Service Co. LTD. offers a wide range of logistics services, including domestic and international shipping, freight forwarding, customs clearance, warehousing, and supply chain management solutions.

How can I track my shipment?

We provide a user-friendly online tracking system that allows you to track your shipment in real-time. Simply enter the tracking number provided to you, and you’ll have access to the latest updates on the status and location of your shipment.

Are there any restrictions on the types of goods you can ship?

While we handle a diverse range of shipments, there are certain restrictions based on legal and safety considerations. Hazardous materials, perishable goods, and items prohibited by local regulations are some examples. Please consult our team for specific details regarding your shipment.

How do you ensure the security of my shipment?

We prioritize the security of your shipment throughout the entire transportation process. We employ strict security measures, such as sealed containers, tracking systems, and verified carriers. Additionally, we work with trusted partners and adhere to industry best practices to mitigate any risks.

Can you handle customs clearance for international shipments?

Yes, we have extensive experience in customs clearance procedures for international shipments. Our team is well-versed in handling the necessary documentation, duties, and taxes to ensure a smooth customs clearance process.

How do you calculate shipping costs?

Shipping costs are determined by various factors, including the weight and dimensions of the shipment, the origin and destination locations, the mode of transportation, and any additional services required. Our team will provide you with accurate and competitive pricing based on these factors.

What is the estimated delivery time for my shipment?

Delivery times depend on several factors, such as the distance, transportation mode, customs processes, and any unforeseen circumstances. We strive to provide you with realistic and reliable delivery estimates based on our expertise and historical data.

Can I insure my shipment?

Yes, we offer shipment insurance to provide coverage against loss, damage, or theft during transit. Our team can guide you through the insurance options available and help you choose the most suitable coverage for your shipment.

How can I contact your customer support team?

You can reach our customer support team via phone, email, or through our website’s contact form. We have a dedicated team ready to assist you with any inquiries, concerns, or specific requirements you may have.

What happens if there are delays or issues with my shipment?

 In the event of any delays or issues, our dedicated customer support team will proactively communicate with you, provide regular updates, and work to resolve the situation promptly. We prioritize customer satisfaction and strive to find satisfactory solutions for any unexpected challenges.

Safeway courier Service Co. Ltd is a full-service logistics provider specializing in ocean freight forwarding, customs brokerage, warehousing, and distribution services.
